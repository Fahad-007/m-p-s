Messaging

Automated

Allows enterprise to send automated bulk SMS messages from their business systems using their existing toll-free number and receive replies. When used with WEBTEXT Contact Center Messaging products, something really smart happens: message replies – depending on their content – can either be forwarded to a contact center chat agent or to the business system.

Manual

Allows enterprise to send and receive bulk messages manually. Messages are sent via WEBTEXT cloud-based messaging gateway using a toll-free number.

Voice Agent Messaging

Enables Voice Agents in real time to identify a cell phone caller and text them information while they’re speaking. Agent also has a complete history of all messaging sent to / from a caller’s number.
